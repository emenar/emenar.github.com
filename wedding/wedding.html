The Wedding of Rebecca Shen and Mark Fussell

    

Rebecca Yin-Shi Shen and Mark Lewis Fussell were married on July Second, Nineteen hundred and ninety nine. This was the culmination of fifteen years together (we are a bit cautious about these things), but the wedding did occur in the same millenium as when we first met.

The ceremony and main reception were held at Kohl Mansion in Burlingame California in July. A second reception was held in Philadelphia in August. Finally, Mark and Rebecca went to Taiwan in October to meet the rest of Rebecca's family. All counted, more than a hundred people attended the ceremony or one of the receptions. Only three of us were at all locations.

  • California
  • Pennsylvania
  • Taiwan
We hope everyone enjoyed the celebrations and that all of our futures together will be even happier than our past. Enjoy the photos.

Rebecca and Mark
