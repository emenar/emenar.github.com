The Wedding of Rebecca Shen and Mark Fussell (Pennsylvania)

    

Rebecca Yin-Shi Shen and Mark Lewis Fussell were married on July Second, Nineteen hundred and ninety nine. A second reception was held in Pennsylvania at Waynesborough Country club, for anyone who could not make it to California. About thirty friends and family came to Pennsylvania, and a few people came to both.
